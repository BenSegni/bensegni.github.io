Current Project

Technical Design Lead, British Airways

Community Collaboration

As part of a high-level initiative to bring greater alignment to practice within the product teams, I am tasked with organising processes to ensure that each product situated in the Help Community endeavours to follow similar ways-of-working when considering QA, development patterns, and pipeline configuration for CI/CD.

The Front-End Dev Talk initiative has brought a great deal of value to the organisation, with an initial group of 7 developers attending the meet-up, now growing to 27 from across the British Airways development group.

Help Centre

Assisting the Help Team, my task has been to guide the Key Developer in organising solid QA strategies for local development and remote CI/CD processes, exploring architectural and technical solutions for the front end. In addition, I have been tasked with generating POC to ensure that features have been suitably researched and tested.

Permissions Centre

Working alongside the Key Developer, my role is to align the Web2 Application to current technologies, drive testing standards and deliver any outstanding features as set out by the product specification.

In addition, to bring greater transparency to progress, my role also includes project management traits, with the organisation of weekly sprint planning, bi-daily stand-ups and demonstration to key stakeholders.

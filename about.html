About

Hi, I'm Ben! I'm from County Durham, and I am a Lead Software Engineer.

I started my career as a Web Designer in 2008, through a passion for design and computers. My university degree was in Architectural Studies, and from there, I was able to combine creative thinking and a laptop to visualise my ideas.

In 2010, I registered Self-Employed and have successfully delivered design and development projects for clients across the globe, which includes Velux, Lenovo, and Amazon.

In 2015, working as a Lead UX Designer for a SAAS platform in Newcastle-upon-Tyne, I began working with AngularJS and have since continued using the framework, leading development teams for clients such as British Airways, Mercedes-Benz, and Kriminalomsorgen.
